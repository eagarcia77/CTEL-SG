FAQ frecuentes

Quick answers to support faculty and reduce repeated questions.

Can I request a workshop for my department?

Sí. En “Service type” selecciona “Solicitud de taller” y explica el tema, público y fecha sugerida.

Does the repository include editable templates?

Yes. The goal is to provide “ready-to-use” documents (Word/PDF/HTML) so you don’t start from scratch.

How are requests assigned?

Puedes elegir una persona en el formulario o dejarlo en “Any CETL team member” para asignación interna.
